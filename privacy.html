Privacy Policy · Last updated: April 2026

Blockade does not collect, store, or share any personal data. This app works fully offline and does not require an account or internet connection.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Blockade ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use our mobile application available on iOS and Android. We are committed to protecting your privacy. In short: we collect nothing.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, transmit, store, or share any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email address, phone number)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage data or analytics
  • Game progress or statistics
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data
  • Cookies or tracking technologies

3. No Internet Connection Required

Blockade is a fully offline game. No internet connection is required to play. The App does not make any network requests, does not connect to external servers, and does not send any data anywhere.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or social media plugins. There are no third parties involved in the delivery of this App who have access to user data.

The App is distributed through the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. Their respective privacy policies govern any data those platforms may collect independently of the App:

  • Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

5. Children's Privacy

Blockade is suitable for all ages. Since the App does not collect any personal data from any users, it is fully compliant with regulations applicable to apps directed at children, including COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

6. Data Security

Because we do not collect any data, there is no user data to secure, breach, or lose. All game state (such as current match progress) is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted.

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to access, correct, and delete personal data. Since we do not collect any personal data, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete. If you have questions, please contact us.
